about me

Hello! I am Lejla Colakovic, a 21-year old multiemedia designer.

My passion for photography started when I was just 13 years old. As I grew older, I got interested in photoediting. I always had a dream about being a professional photographer, but instead, I chose to keep it as a hobby. Then me and my best friend started to make music together. She sang and wrote song, while I would film and edit her music videos and photos. That is where my dream about working within the music industry began.

Now I am making my hobbies and dreams come true as a multimedia designer.

I am not only proficient within video- & photography. I also have experience with graphic design and webdesign. I currently study as multimedia designer and every day, I am learning something new, and developing my skills.
